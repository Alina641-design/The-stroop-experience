Stroopwafel #1

About:

Hidden in the cozy streets of Amsterdam, Stroopwafel #1 celebrates true Dutch simplicity. Each waffle is handmade, warm, and filled with rich caramel — no fancy toppings, just authentic flavor and local charm in every bite.

Golden Perfection:

Each waffle is pressed to a perfect crisp — thin, golden, and lightly caramelized. It’s that melt‑in‑your‑mouth texture that keeps people coming back for more.

Freshly Made Waffles:

Crisp, warm, and filled with golden caramel — each stroopwafel is baked by hand for that true Dutch taste.

Wrapped with Care:

From neatly tied gift packs to classic tins, every stroopwafel is packed with a touch of Dutch charm — perfect to share or to keep as a sweet reminder of Amsterdam.

Sweet Gifts to Take Home:

Packed with care and ready to travel, these stroopwafels make a perfect souvenir from the heart of Amsterdam.
