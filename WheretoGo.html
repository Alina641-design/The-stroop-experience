The Stroop Experience

Firma Stroop:

Pros:

  1. Historic bakery since 1921.
  2. Authentic Dutch stroopwafels.
  3. Cultural experience.
  4. Gluten‑free options available.

Cons:

  1. Fewer topping options.

Perfect for those who want a classic, authentic Dutch stroopwafel steeped in history, with the added benefit of gluten‑free options and freshly baked wafels made right in front of you.

Van Wonderen:

Pros:

  1. Wide topping variety.
  2. Central Amsterdam location.
  3. Tourist‑friendly & photogenic.

Cons:

  1. Can be crowded.
  2. Slightly higher prices.

Go here for a colorful, customizable, tourist‑friendly experience with plenty of topping variety and great photo opportunities.

Stroopwafel #1:

Pros:

  1. Freshly baked on site.
  2. 19 toppings option.
  3. Casual and fun.
  4. Souvenir‑friendly.

Cons:

  1. Smaller shop.
  2. Less historic vibe.
  3. Prices less transparent.

Ideal for a quick treat while exploring, trying creative flavors, or picking up a sweet gift.
